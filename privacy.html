We collect personal information such as name, contact information, and other relevant details necessary for enrollment purposes. This information is provided voluntarily by you.

The information collected is used solely for enrollment and administrative purposes related to Camp General Emilio Aguinaldo. It may also be used to communicate with you regarding updates, events, or important notices.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your personal information. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties unless we provide you with advance notice. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Consent

By using our online enrollment system, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Statement and agree to the collection and use of your personal information as described herein.

Changes to Privacy Statement

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Statement at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Statement periodically for any updates.
